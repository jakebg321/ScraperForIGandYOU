Features

    • All-in-One Convenience: Download, remove metadata, and process videos with one tool—no more juggling multiple platforms.
    • Full Channel Download: Scrape entire YouTube channels or hand-pick your favorite videos for a more tailored approach.
    • Original Content Ready: Automatic metadata removal ensures your videos are treated as unique by social media algorithms.
    • Batch Processing: Save time by downloading multiple videos in one go, whether it's a full page of Instagram Reels or a selection of YouTube Shorts.
    • Pause & Resume: Flexibility to pause and resume downloads at any time, giving you complete control.
    • Real-Time Progress Updates: Stay informed with live progress tracking, ensuring you're always in the loop.

Note: On first run, the application will download necessary components (approximately 150MB). This is a one-time download required for video processing capabilities.

Requirements: Windows 10 or later, Internet connection, 500MB free disk space
